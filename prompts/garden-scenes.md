# Moon Gate Journey Illustration Prompts

## Shared Art Direction

Use this fixed direction for every generated scene:

```text
Use case: historical-scene
Asset type: premium mobile-first interactive learning-game environment illustration
Primary request: Depict one imagined classical Jiangnan scholar garden as a coherent series of changing views.
Rendering: a master-level hand-painted digital illustration, blending precise gongbi architectural linework with translucent Chinese ink wash and delicately layered mineral pigments on warm, fibrous rice paper. Controlled brush marks, graceful negative space, subtle atmospheric depth; not a photograph.
Garden identity continuity: white plaster garden walls with charcoal-gray sweeping tiled eaves; a dark pine-wood waterside pavilion; pale sculptural Taihu limestone rocks; a quiet lotus pond; bamboo and old pine; an occasional circular moon gate or refined geometric lattice window. It must clearly feel like the same garden throughout the series.
Palette continuity: luminous rice-paper ivory, pine green, celadon and lake teal, mineral-blue shadows, restrained cinnabar lotus blossoms, tiny aged-gold sunlight accents. Rich but sophisticated color, never monochrome.
Lighting: soft early-autumn morning after light rain, pearly sky and subtle water reflections.
Composition: no text in the painting and no people as main subjects. Reserve calm, low-detail space where English UI overlays are specified.
Avoid: modern objects, signage or written characters, Western furniture, photographic realism, 3D-render gloss, tourist-poster saturation, random architectural mixtures, watermark or border.
```

## Primary and Learning Views

### `hero-moon-gate.png`

```text
Vertical 4:5 hero view from a shadowed white-plaster corridor through a grand circular moon gate. Beyond it: a celadon pond in the lower middle, the recognizable pine-wood pavilion at right, Taihu rocks and bamboo in layered distance, with a few cinnabar lotus touches. Deep visual invitation into the gate. Keep the lower-left foreground quietly washed and uncluttered for title typography.
```

### `learn-framed-vastness.png`

```text
Vertical 4:5 lesson view for Contrast and Balance. Very close foreground: an elegant dark geometric lattice window in a white wall occupies the left half, with a deliberately small opening. Through that restrained opening is a remarkably deep, luminous pond vista: distant pavilion, rockery and receding trees. Make the small-to-vast spatial contrast instantly legible. Keep the lower area gentle for a reading panel.
```

### `learn-poetic-reflection.png`

```text
Vertical 4:5 lesson view for Poetic and Artistic Refinement. The same waterside pavilion stands quietly beside a still pond; its upturned eaves and Taihu rock reflect clearly in the water like a painted poem. Include a small blank wooden plaque surface under the pavilion eave but absolutely no characters. Warm side light, lotus leaves, broad reflective water with low visual detail for UI.
```

### `learn-changing-view.png`

```text
Vertical 4:5 lesson view for Diversity and Harmony. A winding stone path curves from shaded foreground behind layered bamboo and a partial white corridor wall, then reveals the familiar pavilion and glinting pond only in the distance. Alternating sun and shadow, concealment and revelation, multiple depth planes. Leave bottom-right calmer for UI.
```

### `learn-naturalness.png`

```text
Vertical 4:5 lesson view for Naturalness and Simplicity. The familiar modest pavilion nestles into an irregular natural pond edge with pale Taihu rocks, old pine and mist-soft bamboo; architecture is subordinate to water and stone. Mist rises softly above open water. A tranquil uncluttered lower foreground supports overlaid text.
```

## Game Comparison Views

All paired images below must use matching viewpoint, lighting, crop, garden identity and painterly quality so the student judges spatial aesthetics, not image attractiveness.

```text
Landscape 4:3 game choice for Contrast and Balance, correct option. Restrained foreground, expansive suggested world beyond; lyrical negative space.
```

Landscape 4:3 counterpart to play-frame-a with identical light and same garden elements, incorrect option.
